Who we are..

Shatha Altowai and Saber Bamatraf, both Yemeni artists, are a powerful duo whose artistic and cultural activism reflects their shared commitment to advocating for humanitarian causes, cultural rights, and the arts. Currently based in Edinburgh, Scotland, their creative journeys intersect not only in their personal lives but also in their artistic endeavors, which have been shaped by the turmoil of their home country, Yemen, and their bold stand against societal and political challenges.
